# Code of Conduct

This project is intended to be a focused, respectful engineering space.

Participants are expected to:

- Treat other contributors with professional respect.
- Keep criticism specific to code, docs, tests, behavior, or product decisions.
- Avoid harassment, threats, personal attacks, and discriminatory language.
- Handle security-sensitive information carefully and never post secrets or
  private logs in public channels.

Maintainers may remove disruptive content and may restrict participation when a
participant repeatedly acts against these expectations.
